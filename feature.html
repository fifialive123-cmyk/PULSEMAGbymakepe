How Modern Culture is Reshaping the Way We Live and Work

By Palesa Mokoena March 15, 2026 8 min read

The world around us is changing at an unprecedented pace. From the way we communicate to how we earn a living, the cultural shifts of the 21st century are leaving no aspect of life untouched. Remote work, once a rare perk, has become a standard expectation for millions of workers worldwide.

Digital communities are replacing traditional social circles, and creativity is being redefined by technology. Young people in cities like Maseru, Nairobi, and Lagos are building businesses, movements, and identities entirely online — and the impact is profound.

Our editorial team spent three months exploring how these changes play out in real lives across Southern Africa. What we found challenges many assumptions about tradition, modernity, and what it means to thrive in the digital age.

The Rise of Artificial Intelligence in Everyday Life

By James Molefe March 10, 2026 6 min read

Artificial intelligence is no longer the subject of science fiction — it has arrived in our homes, schools, and workplaces. From smart assistants that manage our schedules to AI-powered tools that help students write and learn, the technology is woven into the fabric of daily life.

But with opportunity comes responsibility. Educators, policymakers, and technologists are grappling with questions about fairness, access, and the long-term effects of relying on machines to make decisions that affect human lives.
